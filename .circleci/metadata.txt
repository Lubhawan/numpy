This dataset contains crime incident reports from the Chicago Police Department from 2001 to present. 
The data includes detailed information about each crime incident including:

- Crime classification (Primary Type, Description, IUCR codes)
- Temporal information (Date, Year, Updated On)
- Location details (Block, District, Ward, Community Area, GPS coordinates)
- Case details (Case Number, FBI Code)
- Incident characteristics (Arrest made, Domestic violence indicator)
- Geographic coordinates (Latitude, Longitude, X/Y Coordinates)

This dataset is commonly used for crime analysis, public safety research, and urban planning.
The data is updated regularly and maintained by the Chicago Police Department.
Contains 50,267 records with 21 fields per record.
